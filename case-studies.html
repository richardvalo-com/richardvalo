Case study

Automated online presentation funnel for 1,300 participants

Online event Automation Access control Ticket bundles Live promo Replay delivery
Context

Online presentation event with a moderator + 5 speakers, built to scale participant handling and reduce manual operations.

  • Single funnel serving a large audience
  • Multiple purchase scenarios including bundles
  • High trust requirements for access delivery
Goal & KPI
  • Automate checkout → invoices → email journeys → unique access links
  • Support bundle sales (e.g., 6 seats) with participant assignment
  • Protect access against duplicate redemption
  • Enable in-event promo with deadline logic
  • Sell replay and deliver automatically after purchase
Solution architecture

Flow: Landing → Checkout → Payment + Invoice → Smart segmentation → Access link delivery → Reminders → Live promo → Replay sales → Automated delivery

  • Unique access links: generated and matched to participant identity
  • Bundles: purchase of multiple seats triggers an assignment flow and tracks redemption per seat
  • Duplicate protection: redemption tracking prevents re-use and handles edge cases
  • Error handling: clear fallback paths for missing data, duplicates, and payment anomalies
  • Promo during event: next event offer + deadline logic (until midnight)
  • Replay sales: post-event automation delivers recording access instantly
What mattered most
  • Clean data structure from the first touchpoint
  • Reliable delivery under pressure (live event)
  • Clear ownership and operational visibility
Next iteration
  • Expand KPI reporting with event-stage dashboards
  • Refine segmentation for replay conversion
  • Reduce support load through proactive edge-case messaging
